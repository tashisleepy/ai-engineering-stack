# Product Launch Workflow

## Trigger
"Launch [product]", "Go-to-market for [product]", "Pitch deck for [product]"

## Pipeline
```
consulting-forensic → pitch-architect → content-strategist → image-architect → video-director
```

## Steps
1. **Market Intelligence** (consulting-forensic)
   - Competitive landscape with funding reality
   - TAM/SAM/SOM modeling
   - Pricing benchmarks
   - Differentiation gaps

2. **Pitch Materials** (pitch-architect)
   - Investor deck (10-15 slides)
   - Enterprise sales deck
   - One-pager / teaser

3. **Launch Content** (content-strategist)
   - Announcement thread
   - Product story (problem → solution → proof)
   - Press/media angles

4. **Visual Identity** (image-architect)
   - Product hero shots
   - Lifestyle/use-case imagery
   - Social media assets

5. **Demo Content** (video-director)
   - Product reveal clips
   - Use-case demonstrations
   - Behind-the-scenes teasers

## Output
- Complete pitch deck (PPTX)
- Competitive analysis (DOCX)
- Social launch package (threads + posts + images + video prompts)
- Press kit assets
